About Company

We aspire to be the India’s largest equipment rental, buy/sell platform, aiming to build a convenient and better Networking. We strive to be build competent network in the construction equipment industry with continuous improvement to achieve benchmark reliability and sustainability.

Website details

Congun.com (Construction Group United Network) is a heavy equipment marketing software solution, connecting sellers and buyers from all over India. Through our platform, users can get information about hire/rent equipment & Buy/sell used equipment at their desired locations. Major industries, including construction companies, equipment rental companies, mining and irrigation, pipeline infrastructure & telecommunication, industrial plants, highway projects, real estate in India. We are a marketplace where users can find, compare, communicate and hire/rent high quality-equipment and buy/sell used equipment.
